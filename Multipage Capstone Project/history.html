TOYOTA Home Specs History
1993 The Toyota Supra MK4 was introduced, featuring both the base model and the Turbo trim, captivating enthusiasts with its powerful performance and sleek design.
1995 Toyota unveiled the limited-edition "RZ-S" model, adding unique features and styling elements to the Supra lineup, further enhancing its appeal among enthusiasts.
1996 The "Aerotop" edition was introduced, showcasing a distinctive removable roof panel, providing a convertible-like driving experience while maintaining the Supra's structural integrity.
2002 Production of the Toyota Supra MK4 concluded, marking the end of an era for one of the most iconic sports cars ever produced, leaving a lasting legacy in automotive history.
